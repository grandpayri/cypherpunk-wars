Protocol: CYPHERPUNK WARS

A decentralized strategy simulation built on the Kaspa BlockDAG.

01. What is Cypherpunk Wars?

It is a competitive "Persistent Browser Game" (PBBG). You take on the role of a rogue Operator trying to dominate the network by managing resources, expanding data centers, and deploying malicious code against rivals.

02. How do I play?

  • EXPAND: Use your turns (Entropy) to build Data Centers. More centers mean more room for Daemons.
  • SPAWN: Convert your $KAS into Daemons—digital soldiers that defend your node and attack others.
  • RESEARCH: Discover new "Hacks" (Spells) to disrupt enemy networks or shield your own.
  • CYBER-ATTACK: Launch your Daemons at a rival Operator to steal their data centers or crash their node.

03. Why Kaspa?

Traditional games use centralized servers. Cypherpunk Wars uses Kaspa’s high-speed BlockDAG to store game states and transactions, ensuring that no single entity can "shut down" the war. It is an unstoppable game for an unstoppable network.

04. Legacy & Credits

Cypherpunk Wars is a modern reimagining and "riff" on the classic 1990s web game Archmage. We stand on the shoulders of giants. While our mechanics are adapted for the blockchain era, we owe our strategic DNA to the original creators of the Archmage system.
