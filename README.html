CYPHERPUNK WARS v1.0 MANIFEST

I. THE WARLORD'S GUIDE

To crush your enemies in Cypherpunk Wars, you must master the delicate balance of economy and entropy.

1. Factions & Units

Every Operator commands 7 Unit Slots. These range from low-tier Daemons (cannon fodder) to high-tier Cypher-Lords. Diversity is keyâ€”massing a single unit type makes you vulnerable to specific counter-spells.

2. War Mechanics

  • PHISH: Low-risk scavenging for $PUNKW fragments.
  • ATTACK: High-risk maneuvers to seize opponent Sectors.
  • ARMAGEDDON: The final victory condition. Triggers a server-wide reset and increments the Season counter.

3. Spells & Research

Researching the 25 Unique Spells requires $PUNKW and Entropy. Spells provide passive buffs to units or active debuffs to rival Operators.

4. Items & Artifacts

Up to 25 Artifacts can be found or forged. Unlike spells, items are persistent and often grant unique abilities like Entropy Shielding.

II. THE ARCHITECT'S PROTOCOL

We leverage the Kaspa BlockDAG to create a sovereign, verifiable game state.

1. vProgs Philosophy

This SPA operates as a vProg (Verifiable Program). Game logic executes off-chain in your browser, but state transitions are committed to the DAG. This provides:

  • Persistence: Your "save" is a transaction.
  • Transparency: No hidden "god-mode" for devs.
  • Composability: Other vProgs can read your $PUNKW balance.
