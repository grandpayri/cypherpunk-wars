[MAN_02] SYSTEM_ARCHITECTURE

01. DECENTRALIZED_STACK

Cypherpunk Wars is a "Serverless" decentralized application (dApp). There is no central database. All game state—including your sectors, $PUNKW balance, and research progress—is derived from the Kaspa BlockDAG.

02. KASPA-WASM_ENGINE

Your browser runs a WebAssembly (WASM) instance of the Kaspa core. This allows your bunker to perform 256-bit cryptographic signing and DAG interaction locally. Your 24-word seed phrase never leaves your machine.

03. DATA_PERSISTENCE (VPROGS)

Game actions are broadcast as transactions with specialized OP_RETURN memos. The game client scans these "vprogs" (Virtual Programs) to reconstruct the global state of the war in real-time.

04. LOCAL_STORAGE_CACHE

While the truth lives on the DAG, your browser uses localStorage to cache your Bunker ID and UI preferences for rapid session resumption.
