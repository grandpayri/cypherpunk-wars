GWh (Gigawatt-Hours)

The energy required to broadcast data to the Kaspa BlockDAG. Every action consumes 1 GWh, which is drawn from your KAS reserves.

Cycles

Your AI cluster's compute capacity. 1 Cycle represents 1 execution unit of work.

Sectors

Physical server rack infrastructure. Expanding your sectors increases the efficiency and yield of your $PUNKW ingestion.

The Armageddon Meta

A global reset initiated by an operator. The season ends when a high-level hack successfully executes a total system wipe.
