Protocol: Hack the Planet

Operator, your mission is simple: Establish your Bunker, secure your Sectors, and dominate the BlockDAG.

1. The Bunker Handshake

Your first step is forging your High-Entropy Identity. This 24-word phrase is your master key.

  • NEVER SHARE THESE WORDS. If you lose them, your Bunker and all its assets are lost to the void forever.
  • Your identity generates your unique Operator ID (Public Address) for receiving Fuel and $PUNKW fragments.

2. Resources: Fuel & Entropy

Survival in the CypherPunk Wars requires two primary energy sources:

  • Fuel ($KAS): Used for industrial upgrades and securing long-term storage of your state on the DAG.
  • Entropy: Your tactical action potential. Every turn—whether gathering fragments or attacking a node—consumes Entropy.
  • Rationing: You receive 10 free Entropy turns per day. You can surge your potential by purchasing up to 100 extra turns for just 1 $KAS.

3. Protocol 01: Phish Network

The primary method for accumulating wealth. Use your Entropy to scan the network and gather $PUNKW Fragments. These fragments can be forged into advanced Daemons or traded with other Operators.

4. Expansion: Sectors & Daemons

The war is won through territory.

  • Build Infra: Spend $KAS to upgrade your Bunker's efficiency and defense.
  • Attack Node: Deploy Daemons to seize Sectors from rival Operators.
  • MMO Persistence: Your Sectors generate passive income as long as you have the Fuel to defend them.
