ABSTRACT: This document outlines the transition of the legacy "Archmage" systems into a fully decentralized, DAG-native warfare protocol. By leveraging the speed and security of Kaspa, we enable an autonomous game state that exists entirely within the BlockDAG, removing the need for central authority or fragile web servers.
01. The Vision

Cypherpunk Wars is more than a game; it is an experiment in digital permanence. By utilizing "vprogs" (Virtual Programs) stored in transaction memos, we create a persistent world that survives as long as the network itself.

02. Proof of Energy

In this ecosystem, every action represents a physical expenditure of network fuel (KAS). This ensures that every Phish, every Attack, and every Sector expansion has real-world weight. We replace "Time" with "Energy" (GWh) as the primary currency of progress.

03. The Social Contract

The protocol is designed to be self-sustaining. Through a community-driven "Cistern" or manual Peer-to-Peer fuel transfers, the barrier to entry is lowered by the success of the established veteran operators. As one bunker grows, it provides the exhaust (energy) needed to ignite the next.
