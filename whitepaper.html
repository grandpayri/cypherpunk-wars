CYPHERPUNK WARS v1.0

An On-DAG Decentralized Massively Multiplayer Turn-Based Strategy War Game

I. ORIGINATION & THE ARCHMAGE LEGACY

CypherPunk Wars is a modern reimagining of the classic Archmage strategy genre. It preserves the deep tactical satisfaction of turn-based territory management while replacing centralized server authority with cryptographic sovereignty.

  • The Bunker: Each player's command center is anchored by a 24-word High-Entropy identity.
  • Entropy Management: Players manage a finite recovery of Entropy to execute tactical operations.

II. THE KASPATHON GENESIS

Born from the Kaspathon, this project serves as a flagship for decentralized application (dApp) innovation on the Kaspa BlockDAG.

CypherPunk Wars embodies the Kaspathon ideals by moving beyond simple transactions to create a complex, state-driven ecosystem that highlights Kaspa's technical superiority.

III. THE DAG AS PERSISTENT STORAGE

Unlike traditional games that rely on ephemeral SQL databases, CypherPunk Wars utilizes the Kaspa BlockDAG as its Immutable Storage Layer.

Sovereign State: Every infrastructure upgrade, $PUNKW yield, and Sector claim is etched into the DAG.

Historical Verification: The BlockDAG maintains a prune-resistant history, ensuring every action can be verified from its cryptographic origin, making the game world "immortal".

IV. VPROGS: THE RULES OF WAR

The roadmap for CypherPunk Wars centers on vProgs (Virtual Programs) as the ideal solution for complex game logic.

Collective Agreement & PVP

vProgs act as the trustless, decentralized referee for all PVP encounters. When Operators clash over a Sector, the vProg executes the "Rules of War" on-chain, ensuring a fair outcome based on a collective agreement coded into the DAG architecture.

Chain-of-Event Verification

Each player's state—from Daemon health to resource consumption—is verified through Chain-of-Event Proofs. This allows any node to confirm that the sequence of tactical moves followed the protocol exactly.

Architectural Integrity

The logic used to exercise game events is not just client-side script; it is verified by the Kaspa Architecture itself. This ensures that the "Laws of Physics" within CypherPunk Wars are immutable and globally consistent.

V. PRODUCT VISION

By exercising browser-side WASM, real-time RPC connectivity, and future vProg automation, CypherPunk Wars proves that the Kaspa BlockDAG is the best fit for the next generation of Massively Multiplayer Strategy games.

VI. ECONOMIC PROTOCOLS: NEAR-ZERO FRICTION

CypherPunk Wars introduces a Hyper-Efficient Micro-Economy designed to sustain both the player and the developer ecosystem through Kaspa’s unparalleled throughput.

The 10 $KAS Battery

Because Kaspa transactions are completely affordable, a single deposit of 10 $KAS can support hundreds of thousands of turns. This creates a "Nearly Free-to-Play" environment where the cost of tactical moves is mathematically negligible for the average Operator.

Monetization & Incentivized Development

To ensure continuous support and development of the CypherPunk theater, the following micro-transaction model is implemented via the DAG:

  • Daily Ration: Every Operator receives 10 Free Entropy Turns per day to maintain basic Bunker functionality.
  • Strategic Surge: Operators can purchase up to 100 additional turns per day for a flat fee of 1 $KAS.

This incentivizes the development team to deliver high-value updates, new "Daemon" classes, and Sector expansions while keeping the entry barrier for new players non-existent.
