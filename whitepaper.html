CypherPunk Wars: v1.0 Whitepaper

An On-DAG Decentralized Massively Multiplayer Turn-Based Strategy War Game.

I. The Origination: Archmage Reborn

CypherPunk Wars is a loving "Cypherpunk" riff on the foundational mechanics of Archmage, the legendary web-based fantasy strategy game. Where the original relied on centralized servers to manage turns and territory, CypherPunk Wars decentralizes the "Mage's Tower" into a cryptographically secured Bunker.

  • Entropy as Energy: In place of fixed turns, players manage Entropy to execute "Phish Network" yields or "Build Infra" protocols.
  • Sovereign Infrastructure: Every asset, from $PUNKW fragments to Sector claims, is verified through a user's 24-word High-Entropy Identity.

II. The Kaspathon Genesis

This project was born from the Kaspathon, an initiative dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what is possible on the Kaspa BlockDAG. CypherPunk Wars meets and highlights the core ideals of the Kaspathon by:

  • Open-Source Innovation: Demonstrating how to bridge Browser WASM with real-time Kaspa RPC nodes without a proprietary backend.
  • Scalable Complexity: Showing that Kaspa's high block rate can support complex, turn-based "War Game" logic at scale.
  • Community Empowerment: Creating a framework where "Kaspa Boys" can interact in a shared, decentralized theater.

III. Why Kaspa? The Best-Fit DAG

Traditional blockchains suffer from "The Sequential Bottleneck." Kaspa’s GHOSTDAG protocol allows for the high-frequency interaction required for an MMO environment:

  • Parallel Execution: Unlike traditional chains, Kaspa allows thousands of "Bunker Handshakes" and "Phish Yields" to happen simultaneously without congestion.
  • Zero-Latency Strategy: Player actions are recorded on the DAG in seconds, maintaining the tactical "flow" of a turn-based war game.
  • Future-Proofing with vProgs: The vision for Kaspa’s vProgs (Virtual Programs) perfectly matches the game’s roadmap for "Smart Daemons"—automated defense scripts that live on the DAG.

IV. Exercising the Features

CypherPunk Wars isn't just a game; it is a live exercise of the Kaspa ecosystem:

  • WASM Identity: Uses the Kaspa WASM SDK for on-client key generation.
  • RPC Resilience: Direct communication with public nodes ensures the game is "immortal" and censorship-resistant.
  • Economic Fuel: Integrates real-time $KAS balance monitoring as the prerequisite "Industrial Fuel" for Bunker operations.
